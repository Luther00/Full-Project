Vision & Mission

Cosat Vision statement.

We at COSAT strive for excellence. We aim to create a learning environment that is current, creative and proactive where students are encouraged to engage critically in the areas of Mathematics, Science and Technology with the aim of becoming productive participants within local and global communities.

Our mission.

Our mission is to provide challenging educational opportunities of a high quality for our students who are drawn in and around Khayelitsha, to become extra-ordinary leaders who are competent and confident in their communities and broader society.

Read more

As part of our mission we aim to:

1. Provide opportunities for holistic development of the students
2. Provide a safe learning environment in which students feel empowered to participate in their education
3. Offer individual attention by enriching high performing students and supporting learners at risk
4. Offer an exemplary model of education that is current, creative and proactive
5. Put in place structures, policies and procedures that will support and enrich the teaching and learning process
6. Support and provide opportunities for staff development
7. Encourage parental involvement to bolster the programme of the school
8. Develop environmental awareness and activism
9. Form partnership with community organizations and universities in support if the school and its students, and in support of excellent education

Our values and beliefs include:

1. Mutual respect
2. Democracy
3. Honesty
4. Commitment
5. Punctuality
6. Integrity
7. Sustainability
